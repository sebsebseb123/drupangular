Drupangular

Drupal/AngularJS "proof of concept" site.

  • The Menu - Is being built referncing taxonomy on the drupal site.
  • The "Thumbs Up/Down" App - Click on the link, you'll see a list of "ratings" which are being pulled from a view. The app also creates nodes using "POST".
  • The "Songs" App - Is pulling in data via a view. Click on the "Play" button on a song, and keep browsing the site... it still plays!
  • Multiple controllers - The audio player lives as part of the site "Chrome"... not "Google Chrome" but site chrome, as in the "decoration" that styles every page.
